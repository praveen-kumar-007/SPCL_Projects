Research Areas

Exploring the core domains that define the future of AI.

Natural Language Processing

Teaching machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language. Our work focuses on large-scale language models, semantic understanding, and conversational AI.

Computer Vision

Enabling computers to see and interpret the visual world. We specialize in object detection, image generation, and 3D scene reconstruction from visual data.

Reinforcement Learning

Developing algorithms that allow agents to learn optimal behaviors through trial and error. Applications range from robotics to complex game-playing AIs.
